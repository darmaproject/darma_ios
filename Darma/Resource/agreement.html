Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of our app (Darma Wallet).

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data Collected

Personal data is any kind of data that could identify an individual.

Darma keys and public addresses are collected and processed by the app locally for the purpose of processing transactions and transmitted into the Darma Network in encrypted form.

Other personal data is not collected by the app.

If you use the USD/EUR exchange (optional) functionality, Darma wallet fetches the exchange rate through the public API of exchange.darmacash.com. See their privacy policy at https: details on how data in your requests is collected (especially the section "Information We Collect Automatically").

App Permissions

  • INTERNET : Connect to the Darma Network via a Darma Daemon Node
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE : Read wallet files stored on the device
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE : Write wallet files stored on the device
  • WAKE_LOCK : Keep device awake while syncing
  • CAMERA : Scan QR Codes for receiving Darma

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and on the website (www.darmacash.com) You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

This Privacy Policy was last updated: 2nd November, 2019.
